Tuning Knob Operations, used for both Frequency and RIT

    Tuning POT = Incremental Near Center
    Tuning POT = Auto Near Extremes
    Tuning POT = Faster Auto At Extremes 

Button Operations

    Btn1 Single Press = Toggle RIT Mode ON/OFF
    Btn1 Double Press = Swap A/B VFO's
    Btn1 Long Press = VFO Reset
    Btn2 = Left, Move Frequency Cursor
    Btn3 = Right, Move Frequency Cursor
    Btn4 = Select: USB, LSB, or Auto USB (default)
    Btn5 = Up, Change HAM Band (save current Freq and SSB Mode)
    Btn6 = Down, Change HAM Band (save current Freq and SSB Mode)
    Btn7 Single Press = Toggle Tune 2500Hz Mode
    Btn7 Double Press = Set Dial Calibration, from RIT value
